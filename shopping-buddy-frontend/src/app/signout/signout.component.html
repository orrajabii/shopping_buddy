Add a Shop

Add a shop to add your own products and sell them using Shopping Buddy.



Benefits of creating your own Shop


  1. The only place to escape competitor advertising on Shopping Buddy

    2. You should view your Shopping Buddy store as a sanctuary away from competitor advertising on the platform. Any seller who has advertised on Shopping Buddy knows just how permeated Shopping Buddy listings are with PPC opportunities.

  2. Shows off all your inventory

    3. Creating an Shopping Buddy store is a great opportunity to allow Shopping Buddy shoppers to see your entire range in one place, unlike those without one, your products can be differentiated from one another rather than presented as a jumbled list.

  3. Helps launch new products successfully

    4. New product launches can be costly regarding advertising on Shopping Buddy because you must actively build Shopping Buddy SEO ranking from scratch. Creating a Store is free however, and provides a platform to advertise your new range for no extra cost other than Sponsored Brand ads.

  4. Adds some pizazz to your Shopping Buddy account

    5. A personal touch can really go a long way when selling online. Imagine that you had the choice to buy from a company page that looked the same as everyone else’s or one that appeared unique. Chance are that you would at least look at the unique company’s products out of curiosity.

  5. Drives Brand Affinity

    6. Further to adding personality to your brand, your Shopping Buddy store page can also provide you with the opportunity to create customer loyalty. What content you choose to promote on your brand page can help you connect with your customers through shared common values.

  6. Brings consumers from off-site to your Shopping Buddy pages

    7. Creating an Shopping Buddy store also provides you with a static url that you can use on any of your social posts so that you can drive customers to your ASINs. It is a good idea to send shoppers to your Shopping Buddy account to make purchases as the more conversions you make, the higher your ranking becomes.
